After my fairly extensive review, here is the list of algorithms I am
personally still quite interested in, by category.  Note that I
repeated Yescrypt in each category it competes in:

Authentication Servers
- ----------------------
EARWORM (if fixed)
Yescrypt

GPU Defense (bcrypt-like)
- -------------------------
BattCrypt
POMELO
PufferFish
Yescrypt

Large Memory-Hashing (Scrypt-like)
- ----------------------------------
Lyra2
TwoCats
Yescrypt

Cache-Timing Resistant
- ----------------------
Catena
Gambit

Great Ideas*
- ------------
AntCrypt
Makwa
Parallel
Schvrch**

* PolyPassHash is a great idea, but not directly a password hashing
scheme or hashing function
** I have not read about Schvrch's algorithm, so I'm taking other
people's word that this is a great idea.
